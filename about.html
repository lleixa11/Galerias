Our Story

Galerias Mobile Pet Grooming was founded in 2018 by passionate animal lovers who saw a need for stress-free, high-quality grooming services in Miami. What started as a single van has grown into a fleet of 3 custom-equipped mobile units serving thousands of happy pets across Miami-Dade and Broward counties.

Our name "Galerias" reflects our belief that every pet deserves a gallery-worthy grooming experience - one that combines artistry, care, and professionalism. We've built our reputation on trust, transparency, and a genuine love for animals.

Our Mission

To deliver the most convenient, gentle, and premium grooming experience in Miami. Every pet deserves to feel relaxed and pampered without leaving their comfort zone.

Our Vision

To become Miami's most trusted mobile pet grooming service, setting the standard for quality, compassion, and innovation in pet care.

Our Values

Compassion, excellence, transparency, and respect - for every pet and every pet parent who trusts us with their beloved companion.
