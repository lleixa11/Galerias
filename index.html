About Us ✦

Passion for Pets, Commitment to Quality

Galerias Mobile Pet Grooming was founded in Miami with a simple mission: to provide stress-free, luxury grooming experiences right at your doorstep. Our team of certified groomers has over 15 years of combined experience and a genuine love for animals.

We use only premium, hypoallergenic products and state-of-the-art equipment in our custom-built vans. Every pet receives individualized attention, and we specialize in handling anxious or senior pets with extra care and patience.
